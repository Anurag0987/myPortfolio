Projects
Technologies:

Node JS, Express, Javascript, SQL, AWS, Docker, Python, AI&ML

Description:

In this project, I led the development of backend solutions for both end-user and EMP (Enterprise Mobility Platform) software applications. These applications were designed to efficiently manage, monitor, and access hardware devices and their endpoints.

Technical Contributions:

RESTful APIs: I designed and implemented RESTful APIs to enable seamless communication between the software applications and the hardware devices. These APIs formed the foundation of our platform's functionality.

Integration of 3rd-Party Open Source Services: I successfully configured and integrated various third-party open-source services, including Snort IPS/IDS System, OpenVAS vulnerability scanner system, Asterisk VOIP server, VPN services, and Tableau visualization server. This integration enhanced our platform's capabilities and security.

Custom Instant Messaging Services: I developed custom instant messaging services using web sockets, enabling real-time communication and collaboration among users of the platform.

Cloud Storage Data Management: I created a cloud storage data management service to efficiently store and retrieve data from the cloud, ensuring scalability and data accessibility.

Intermediate Python Scripts: I developed intermediate Python scripts to effectively manage and process data from IoT devices and their responses. These scripts played a crucial role in data processing and analysis.

AWS Services Utilization: Leveraging AWS services such as IoT Greengrass, S3, EC2, RDS, SQS, and Lambda, I ensured the scalability, reliability, and accessibility of our platform.

Research and Development:

Open Source Alternatives: I conducted extensive research and development efforts to identify and implement open-source alternatives for key features in the market. This strategic move enhanced our platform's security and efficiency.

Wireframing and UX: I contributed to wireframing the key features and functionalities of the EMP platform. This process aimed to provide end-users with insightful information, improve the user experience, and make the platform more user-friendly.

Approach:

Requirement Analysis: I began by carefully understanding project requirements, gathering essential information, and defining project objectives.

Design and Evaluation: Next, I designed the initial flow of the project, taking into consideration industry standards and best practices. I evaluated similar use cases in the market to ensure our approach was competitive and innovative.

Development and Optimization: Following the design phase, I executed the development, continuously optimizing and enhancing features for efficiency and scalability.
